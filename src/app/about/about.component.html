When I was an art student I did not understand what painting meant to me. I just painted because I found it easy. After completing College, I took a break to raise my daughter and immersed myself again after she started to attend school.

In my late 40s, I continued to study under a wide range of artists while learning different styles and directions. Now I am comfortable working with most resources.

My future work will utilize more creativity while still allowing me to express my feelings using a wide variety of subject matter.

I am excited...
